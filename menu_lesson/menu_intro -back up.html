Stress and Intonation Pattern

The stress position in Indonesian is relative fixed . The stress usually falls on penultimate syllabe , the syllabe before last

bela béla defend
pembelaan pembelájaran defence
taman táman park

An ultimate syllabe will be stressed when the penultimate syllable contain week wovel

belah belah cut
bekerja bekerja work
