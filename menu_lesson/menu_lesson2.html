A. Percakapan I

Di dalam Penerbangan

On the airplane taking them to Indonesia, a stewardess gave Billy and Jane forms to enter Indonesia.
Pramugari : Permisi, Anda akan berwisata?
   Exucuse me, will you make a tour?
Jane : ya.
   Ya.
Pramugari : Kalau begitu, Anda harus mengisi tiga formulir ini.
    Well, you should fill in this forms then.
Jane : Maaf, yang putih untuk apa?
   Excuse me, what is this the white form for?
Pramugari : Yang putih untuk bea cukai.
    the white one is for the customs.
Billy Yang coklat dan kuning untuk apa?.
   What about the brown and the yellow ones?
Pramugari : Yang coklat untuk imigrasi. Yang kuning untuk catatan kesehatan.
   The brown one is for the immigration. The yellow is for the health report.
Jane : Oh, begitu. Terima kasih.
   Oh i see, Thank you.
Pramugari : Sama-sama.
   You are welcome
Catatan Kebahasaan
Close
(1) Maaf is similiar to the english "sorry" and "excuse me". besides using maaf, the other word that is usually used to excuse is permisi.
(2) The word yang in yang putih untuk bea cukai is used when the thing refers to is the same as it is mentioned before. In this case, yang putih "the white one" refers to formulir putih "white form".

A. Percakapan II

Membuat Visa di bandara

Arriving at Soekarno-Hatta airport, Billy Smith and Jane Fairchild directly headed to the immigration counter to apply visa on arrival.
Petugas : Selamat sore.
   Good afternoon.
Billy : Selamat sore, kami mau membuat visa.
   Good afternoon, we want to apply for visa
Petugas : Siapa nama anda?
    What is your name?
Billy : Saya Billy Smith dari Australia. Ini teman saya, Jane Fairchild.
   I am Billy Smith from Australia. This my friend Jane Fairchild.
Petugas : Boleh lhat paspor anda?
    May i see your passport?
Billy : Ini, Pak.
    Here they are, Sir.
Petugas : Baiklah. Tunggu sebentar.
   All right. Wait a momment please.
Petugas : Oke, ini paspor dan visa anda
   Okay, here are your passpor and visa.
Billy : Terima kasih.
   Thank you
Petugas : Kembali, selamat berwisata
   You are welcome, Have a nice stay
Catatan Kebahasaan
Close
(1) Oke is derived from and english word "okay". In daily conversation, this word is used to show and agreement. oke, has the same meaning as baiklah.
(2) anda is actualy used for both man and woman, the one talk to, without considering social status and age.

C.Tata Bahasa

1. Kata Kerja dengan me-

Kata kerja or acting words in Indonesian are sometimes formed by using me-. for example :

(1) baca → membaca
Billy membaca buku.
Billy Read a book.
(2) buat → membuat
Jane membuat visa.
Jane applied for a visa.
(3) ambil → mengambil
Saya mengambil paspor.
I took a passport.
(4) isi → mengisi
Billy dan Jane mengisi Formulir.
Billy and Jane filled in the form.
(5) inap → menginap
Saya menginap di hotel.
I spent the night in the hotel.
(6) lihat → melihat
Nyoman melihat Pramugari.
Nyoman saw a stewardess.
(7) cari → mencari
Billy mencari Jane.
Billy looked for Jane.
(8) sapu → menyapu
Jane menyapu lantai.
Jane swept the floor.

From the examples , it can be seen that the change of me- into mem-, meng-, men-, and meny- depends on the words attached to it.

2. Kata Kerja dengan ber-

Besides me- in Indonesian can be formed by using ber- .
For the example :

(1) wisata → berwisata
Billy dan Jane berwisata ke Indoesia.
Billy and Jane made a tour to Indonesia.
(2) belanja → berbelanja
Saya berbelanja di pasar.
i shop at the market.
(3) Ajar → belajar
John belajar bahasa indonesia.
John learned Indonesian.
(4) kerja → bekerja
Andi bekerja di kedutaan.
Andi works at the embassy.

similiar to me- , ber- also chandes according to the words that are attached to it . ber- can change into be- and bel-. The meaning of ber- at the above sentences is "to do". Another meaning of acting words with ber- is "to wear" or "to use".

(5) topi → bertopi
Billy bertopi.
Billy is wearing a hat.
(6) sepeda → bersepeda
Jane bersepeda.
Jane is riding bicycle.
(7) kacamata → berkacamata
Saya berkacamata.
I am wearing glasses.
(8) dasi → berdasi
Petugas itu Berdasi.
That attendant is wearing a tie.

3. Kata Kerja dengan di-

Another way to form acting words is indonesian is by using di-.
For the example :

(1) baca → dibaca
novel dibaca Billy.
The novel is read by Billy.
(2) ambil → diambil
Formulir diambil Jane.
The form is taken by Jane.
(3) beli → dibeli
Patung asmat dibeli Jane.
The Asmat statue is bought by Jane

unlike me- , di- does not change its form whatever words are attached to it.


4. Kata tanya siapa

Question word siapa is used for people. Question word siapa is similiar to the english question word who or whom.
For the example :

(1) Q : siapa itu?
Who is that? 		A : Itu Billy.
That is Billy.
(2) Q : siapa dia?
Who is she?		 A : Dia Jane.
She is Jane
(3) Q : siapa belajar bahasa indonesia?
Who learned indonesian? 		A : Sandra belajar bahasa indonesia.
Sandra learned indonesian.
(4) Q : Jane berbicara dengan siapa ?
To whom is Jane Talking? 		A : Jane berbica dengan Billy.
Jane is talking to Billy.
(5) Q : Billy pergi ke Indonesia dengan siapa ?
With whom did Billy go to Indonesia?		 A : Billy pergi ke Indonesia dengan Jane.
Billy went to Indonesia with Jane

Question word siapa can also used to ask about possesiveness. In this case, siapa is similiar to the english question word whose.
For the example :

(6) Q : Ini rumah siapa ?
Whose house is this ? 		A : Ini rumah saya.
This is my house.
(7) Q : Itu paspor siapa ?
Whose passport is that ?		 A : Itu paspor Billy.
that is Billy's passport.

In the daily conversation, the answer to those questions may be short, not fully grammatical forms.
For example :

(8) Q :siapa mereka ?
Who are they? 		A : Temanku.
my friend.
(8) Q : Ini rumah siapa ?
Whose house is this ? 		A : Jane.
Jane.
(9) Q : Jane berbicara denga siapa ?
To WhomJane are talking?		 A : Billy.
Billy.
