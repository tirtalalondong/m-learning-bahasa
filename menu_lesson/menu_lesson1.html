A. Percakapan I

Di kedutaan Besar Indonesia

Billy Smith and Jane Fairchild went to Indonesian Embassy in Canverra, Australia. Right now, they are in front of the counter to apply visa to visit Indonesia.
Petugas : Selamat Pagi.
   Good morning.
Billy : Selamat pagi. Saya Billy Smith. Ini teman saya, Jane Fairchild. Kami mau membuat visa.
   Good morning. I am Billy Smith. This is my friend, Jane Fairchild. We want to apply for visa.
Petugas : Anda pernah ke Indonesia?
   Have u ever visited Indonesia before?.
Billy : Belum.
   Not yet.
Petugas : Boleh saya melihat paspor anda?
   May i see your passport?
Billy : : Ya. ini paspor kami.
   Yes. Here our passport.
Petugas : : Terima kasih. Apa tujuan anda datang ke Indonesia?
   Thank you. What is your purpose of coming to Indonesia?
Billy : Kami mau berwisata.
   We will make a tour.
Petugas : Berapa lama?
   How long?
Billy : Dua minggu.
    Two weeks.
Petugas : Oh. Anda dapat membuat visa langsung di Bandara Soekarno-Hatta.
   Oh. You can apply for it directly at Soekarno-Hatta airport
Billy : Baiklah. Terima kasih.
   All right. Thank you.
Petugas : Sama-sama. Selamat berwisata.
   You are welcome. Have a nice trip.
Catatan Kebahasaan
Close
(1) In a close or an informal conversation selamat pagi is often abbreviated to pagi, as well as selamat siang or selamat malam is also abbreviated to siang or malam.
(2) Bandara is the abbreviation of bandar udara "airport" or. For example, Bandara Soekarno-Hatta means bandar udara Soekarno-Hatta.
(3) In a formal conversation, the expression of terima kasih is said completely. However, In an informal situation terima kasih is often abbreviated to makasih even trims.
(4) In responding to thanking, sama-sama is interchangeable with kembali.
Catatan Budaya
Close
KTP (Kartu Tanda Penduduk) is an Indonesian identity card in which there are the owner identities, such as name, addres, date of birth, marital status, blood type, religion,and occupation.

In Indonesia ,there some words that refer to certain time , those are pagi,siang , sore dan malam. In line with that,the expression of Selamat pagi is commonly used between 04.00-10.00. Selamat siang used between 10.00-14.00. Selamat sore is between 14.00-18.30 , and Selamat malam is between 18.30-04.00. Besides, between 16.00-18.30 the expression of Selamat petang is also used. Meanwhile ,time between 00.01-04.00 is called Dini hari, but the expression of Selamat Dini hari is never used.

Angka

Learning the Indonesian Numbers is very important because its structure is used in every day conversation. The more you master it the more you get closer to mastering the Indonesian language. But first we need to know what the role of Numbers is in the structure of the grammar in Indonesian. Indonesian cardinal number convey the "how many" they're also known as "counting numbers," because they show quantity.

Grammar Tips: In Indonesian numbers from 1 to 20 are unique and therefore need to be memorized individually. Numbers from 21 and upwards are formed by using the following pattern: for example 21 can be formed by using 20 + 1 while connecting them. 22 = dua puluh dua. 45 can be formed by using 40 + dan + 5 while connecting them: 45 = empat puluh lima.

C.Grammar

1. ini dan itu

Ini used to refer something which is close to the speaker. While itu is used to refer to something which is far or quite far from the speaker

for the example :

(1) Ini Paspor.
this is a pasport.
(2) Itu Buku.
that is a book.
(3) Ini Billy.
this is Billy.
(4) Itu Jane.
that is Jane.

2. Kata tanya Apa

Question word Apa can be used to ask thing(s).

(1) Apa itu?
What is that? 		Itu Taksi
That is a Taxi
(2) Apa ini?
what is it?		 ini buku
That is a book

Question word apa in Indonesia can be emphasized by adding-kah to Apakah. This question word can be used to form yes or no question.
for the example :

(3) Apakah anda Billy?
Are u Billy? 		Ya, Saya Billy
Yes , i am Billy
(4) Apakah anda Jane
Are u Jane?		 Bukan , saya Suzan
No , I am not. i am Suzan
(5) Apakah dia polis
Is he a police? 		Ya, dia polisi
Yes , he is a police
(6) Apakah kamu dokter Are u a doctor? Bukan , saya guru
No , i am not. I am a teacher
(7) Apakah ini formlir
Is this a form? 		Ya, ini formulir
Yes , this is a form
(8) Apakah itu paspor Is that a pasport? Bukan , itu dompet
No ,that is not. That is a wallet

Beside using ya or bukan , Question with apa/apakah can also using be answered by using ya or tidak .
For the example :

(9) Apakah Billy mau ke Indonesia?
Did Billy want to go to Indonesia? 		Ya, Billy mau ke Indonesia
Yes , he did
(10) Apakah anda mengisi formulir itu?
Did u fill in that form?		 Tidak , saya tidak mengisi formulir tersebut
No ,i did not
(11) Apakah Jane pergi ke Bali?
Did Jane go to Bali? 		Ya, Jane pergi ke Bali
Yes , she did

Question in Indonesia is not only started by using question words. A simple way to make question Indonesia is by rising the final pitch of statement.
For the example :

(12) Jane pergi ke Bali?
Jane went to bali? 		Ya, Jane pergi ke Bali
Yes , she did
(13) Billy mau ke Indonesia?
Billy Wanted to go to Indonesia ?		 Tidak
No ,i did not

3. Pernyataan

Pernyaataan or statement is used to inform something to the reader or to the listener.

(1) Nama saya Billy Smith.
My name is Billy Smith.
(2) Saya mau ke Indonesia.
I am going to Indonesia.
(3) Billy dan Jane pergi ke Bali.
Billy and Jane went to Bali.
(4) Anton lahir pada tanggal 21 Januari 1978.
i was born on 21st January 1978.
